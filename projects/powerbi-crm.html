BI & decision support

Power BI CRM Dashboard (RFM Threshold Optimiser)

Interactive dashboard to support CRM targeting decisions by balancing audience coverage vs response performance. The report helps select practical thresholds (reach vs efficiency) using lift-style comparisons.

Power BI DAX Targeting Lift Threshold selection

Problem

Business context

CRM teams often need to decide “who to target” based on imperfect signals. This dashboard supports that decision by making the trade-off explicit: as you raise the threshold, the audience shrinks but performance (response rate / lift) typically improves.

What it includes

Dashboard design

  • Threshold selector: compare multiple RFM cut-offs.
  • Coverage vs performance: audience size vs response rate (lift-style comparison).
  • Targeting simulator: choose thresholds for efficiency vs scale.
  • Stakeholder-ready: clear callouts and recommended ranges.

Insights

3 key takeaways

  1. Trade-off is measurable: higher thresholds generally increase efficiency but reduce reach.
  2. One threshold won’t fit all: the “best” cut-off depends on the campaign objective.
  3. Decision support: lift-style views help explain the choice to non-technical stakeholders.

Recommendations

What I would do next

  • Align threshold selection with budget and CRM goal (retention vs acquisition vs reactivation).
  • Operationalise threshold rules and track outcomes over time (lift, ROI, churn).
  • Combine with predictive response scores to refine targeting decisions further.
