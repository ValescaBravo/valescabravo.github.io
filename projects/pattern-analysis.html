Customer Pattern Analysis (Behaviour & Lifecycle)

Business question: What patterns explain customer value concentration and engagement, and how can they guide CRM strategy?

Open full report (HTML) View code (GitHub) Back to home

Data & method

  • Descriptive CRM analysis across spending, products, channels and lifecycle signals.
  • Designed as a stakeholder-friendly narrative to support campaign and retention planning.
  • Insights structured to move from “what we see” to “what to do next”.
Outcome: a set of actionable insights to support retention, cross-sell and channel strategy.

Key insights

  • Customer value is concentrated, supporting tiered CRM (VIP, core, reactivation).
  • Channel preference enables more effective contact policy and personalised messaging.
  • Behavioural patterns highlight clear opportunities for cross-sell and retention triggers.
