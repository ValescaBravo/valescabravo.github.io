About

My background sits at the intersection of marketing operations and data analytics. I spent the early part of my career in digital and programmatic advertising — trading campaigns across Latin America, managing 50+ simultaneous accounts, and building the reporting infrastructure teams relied on for weekly decisions. That grounding in how marketing actually runs shapes the way I work with data: I build for the question behind the question, not just the metric.

More recently I have focused on formalising that practice: a Master's in Big Data & Business Intelligence (NFQ Level 9, 8.1/10), a Higher Diploma in Data Analytics (NFQ Level 8), and a portfolio of applied projects in CRM segmentation, predictive modelling, and marketing mix modelling.
